Our story

Canvas & Co. was born in a small studio in Buenos Aires with a simple belief: great art should be accessible to everyone. We partner with independent artists to bring you original paintings, prints, and sculptures — each with its own story.

Every purchase supports the artist directly. No middlemen, no markups — just pure creativity.
